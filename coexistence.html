Coexistence

Garment design project – independent work, Italy, 2025
Process: textile research, pattern making, paper prototyping

Inspired by a landscape where light, airy elements coexist with darker, compact surfaces, this project explores how contrasting conditions can occupy the same space. Wind, water and time continually shape the landscape through subtle, cumulative actions. Rather than resolving opposites, the jacket invites them to dialogue: lighter, flexible fabrics meet denser, more compact ones in a layered construction. Transitions are gradual, reflecting slow and indirect processes. Working directly on the body through pattern studies and full‑scale paper trials made it possible to test how light and compact elements influence proportion, tension and movement.
