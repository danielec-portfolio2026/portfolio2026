Here Only Here

Urban photography project – independent work, Garbatella (Rome), Italy, 2025
Medium: digital photography

This photographic series documents a recurring sense of calm, warmth and quiet intimacy found in Garbatella, a historic neighbourhood in Rome. Through light, vegetation and a slow human presence, the work portrays spaces that feel ordinary yet function only in this specific configuration – here, and only here. Vegetation is not decorative but structural: gardens and trees soften the built environment, slow movement and create places meant for staying rather than passing through. Walls, paths and courtyards are accessible but never exposed; spaces feel shared without being completely open and intimate without being private. Light, colour and enclosure work together to produce a sense of quiet protection within the public realm.
