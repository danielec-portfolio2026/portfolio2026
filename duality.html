Duality

Photography project – independent work, USA, 2024
Medium: digital photography

This photographic series explores the duality within a single individual – how seemingly different interests or appearances can coexist in one person. By photographing a subject in contrasting environments and attire, the project questions the idea of a fixed identity and celebrates multiplicity. Each composition balances opposites: tenderness and strength, fragility and determination, introspection and expression. The resulting images suggest that duality is not a contradiction but a rich and nuanced state of being.
