Boredom as Creativity – Compositions

Experimental object – independent work, 2026
Materials: acrylic panels, wooden rods

With the simple kit of perforated panels and locking rods, users can assemble endless compositions. Each structure invites exploration of transparency, layering and the play between light and shadow. Building becomes a form of drawing in space, revealing new patterns and rhythms with every variation.
