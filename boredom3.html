Boredom as Creativity – Spatial Experiments

Experimental object – independent work, 2026
Materials: acrylic panels, wooden rods

Beyond simple compositions, the kit encourages spatial experimentation. By building in relation to architecture and environment, the user can create structures that frame views, cast intricate shadows or invite movement. Even a single arrangement sparks new ideas, turning idle moments into opportunities for discovery.
